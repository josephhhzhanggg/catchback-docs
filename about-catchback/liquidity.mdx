- **1:1 USD Value**: CatchCoins maintain exact parity with USD

## Eligible Items

### Protected Cards
- **Condition Requirements**: Near Mint or better condition
- **Authentication**: Verified authentic through our grading partners
- **Market Presence**: Cards with established market history and pricing data

### Protected Sealed Products
- **Factory Sealed**: Original manufacturer packaging intact
- **Storage Standards**: Proper storage conditions maintained
- **Product Categories**: Major sports and gaming products from established manufacturers

### Exclusions
- **Promotional Items**: Certain limited promotional products may not be eligible
- **Damaged Items**: Cards with significant wear, creases, or damage
- **Counterfeit Products**: Any items identified as non-authentic
