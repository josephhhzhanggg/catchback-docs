Every card you pull from a CatchBack pack — and every card you buy on the marketplace — is automatically vaulted in our climate-controlled, insured facility. You don't have to ship anything. You don't have to store anything. The card is yours, fully insured, and ready to act on whenever you want.

## What gets vaulted automatically

- **Cards pulled from packs** — every card from every pack tier
- **Slabs and graded cards** purchased on the marketplace
- **Unopened packs** held in your inventory before you open them

Submission of your own physical cards is currently in beta for approved users.

## What "insured" actually means

### Complete collectibles insurance
Our entire vault is covered by a collectibles insurance policy. Every card you own — at any value — is fully protected against loss, damage, or theft while it's in our facility.

### Climate-controlled environment
Cards degrade fast under typical home conditions. We hold the vault at:

- **Stable temperature** — prevents expansion, contraction, and material breakdown
- **Managed humidity** — prevents warping, curling, and moisture damage
- **Filtered air** — no pollutants or UV exposure

The kind of environment professional graders and museums use for high-value collectibles.

### 24/7 monitored security
Continuous video surveillance with motion alerts and tamper detection. Real-time monitoring with a documented chain of custody for every card from check-in to check-out.

## How vaulting fits with the rest of the platform

Vaulting is the foundation that makes everything else on CatchBack work:

- The **80% CatchBack guarantee** is possible because we already hold the card, so we can verify it instantly and pay out without shipping or grading delays.
- The **$1 marketplace fee** works because cards transfer digitally between vault entries instead of physically shipping for every trade.
- **CatchBack Auctions** can run at full speed because the listed card is already authenticated and in our possession — no "will the seller actually ship it?" risk.

When you do want to take a card out — for a personal collection, a show, or to flip it elsewhere — you request a shipout and we handle the logistics from our end.

## Why we built it this way

Trading cards work best when they're treated like real assets: stored properly, insured, liquid, and easy to value. The vault gives every CatchBack collector the same custodial-grade infrastructure that institutional buyers and serious investors have always had — without the per-card storage fees, the long wait times, or the hassle of shipping cards for every transaction.
