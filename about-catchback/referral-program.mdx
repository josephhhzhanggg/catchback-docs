Every CatchBack account comes with a unique referral code. Share it with friends — when they sign up using your code, they get **10% off their first pack purchase**, and you earn **ongoing commission as CatchCoins** on the purchases they make afterward.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your referral code">
    Open your account settings — your unique referral code is displayed there. You can share it as a link or just paste the code itself.
  </Step>

  <Step title="A friend signs up with your code">
    When they create their account, they enter your code. We track the relationship between the two accounts automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="They get 10% off their first pack">
    Your referee can apply their welcome discount to any pack purchase for **10% off**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="You earn commission as they spend">
    Every time your referee makes a qualifying purchase, you earn commission, paid out in CatchCoins straight to your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you earn

When your referee makes a qualifying purchase, you earn a percentage of that purchase as **CatchCoins** in your wallet. Commission is added automatically — no claim step needed. You can:

- Spend the CatchCoins on more packs or marketplace listings
- [Convert them to USD](/about-catchback/catchcoins) and withdraw

Each referral has its own commission window and rate, both set when the referral is created.

## What your referee gets

- **10% off** their first pack purchase, applied with their welcome code
- A working account with the full platform — packs, marketplace, vault, CatchBack guarantee, all of it
- Their own referral code, which they can share to bring in their own referees

## The referrer leaderboard

We track the top referrers in real time on a public leaderboard, ranked by total commission earned. Top referrers earn additional recognition and rewards — the higher your earnings, the higher you climb.

## Tracking your referrals

Inside your account, you can see:

- **Your unique referral code** and a shareable link
- **Total referrals** — how many people have signed up with your code
- **Total commission earned** — running lifetime total in USD-equivalent
- **Commission history** — per-transaction breakdown of each commission you've earned, including pending and paid status

## Frequently asked

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does my referee have to use the code at signup?">
    Yes. The referral relationship is established when they create their account using your code. Without that, the system has no way to attribute their future purchases to you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What purchases earn commission?">
    Qualifying platform purchases — packs (standard, custom, and specialty), and store credit purchases all count. CatchCoins purchases and free balance adjustments do not.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long do I keep earning commission from one referee?">
    Each referral has a commission window set when the referral is created. After that window ends, the relationship stays tracked but commission stops accruing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When does commission show up in my wallet?">
    Commissions land in your CatchCoins balance shortly after the referee's purchase completes. You'll see them in your wallet history with the originating transaction noted.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I refer myself with a second account?">
    No. Multi-accounting violates platform terms and any commissions or discounts gained that way will be reversed, with potential account consequences.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the program ongoing?">
    The referral program runs as long as it's enabled on the platform. Rates and commission windows may change over time — your existing referrals are honored under the terms in effect when they were created.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Important terms

- **Commission is paid in CatchCoins**, not directly in USD. CatchCoins can be converted to USD through the [cash conversion flow](/about-catchback/catchcoins).
- **Eligibility is at platform discretion.** Accounts found to be in violation of platform terms — including multi-accounting and fraudulent activity — are excluded from referral rewards.
- **Rates and terms can be updated.** CatchBack reserves the right to modify commission rates, discount amounts, and program eligibility going forward. Changes are communicated before they take effect for existing referrals.
