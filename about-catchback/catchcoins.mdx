# CatchCoins

CatchCoins are the digital representation of a user's wallet balance on the CatchBack platform. They are used to purchase packs, participate in auctions, and transact across the CatchBack ecosystem.

## Important Terms

- **No Inherent Value**: CatchCoins have no inherent monetary value. They are a digital balance used solely within the CatchBack platform.
- **All Sales Are Final**: All purchases of CatchCoins are final once credited to your account. No refunds or reversals will be issued.
- **Sole Discretion**: The usage, availability, and terms of CatchCoins are at the sole discretion of CatchBack Cards. CatchBack Cards reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate CatchCoin functionality at any time.
