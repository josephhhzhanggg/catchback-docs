# Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CatchBack, CatchCoins, and our platform.

## Common Questions

<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="How does the CatchBack buyback system work?">
    All eligible cards can be sold back to us for CatchCoins with our guaranteed buyback system. Fresh pack pulls receive an enhanced 90% of market value within 24 hours, while marketplace cards receive 80% of market value - providing competitive liquidity for all your assets.
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="What is the guaranteed catchback rate for cards and sealed product?">
    All protected cards and protected sealed products are eligible for a standard 80% CatchCoins CatchBack, redeemable at any time. This guarantee never expires as long as the item remains in eligible condition. Certain promotional products may not be eligible for the standard CatchBack program.
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="How do CatchCoins work?">
    CatchCoins are our platform currency used for all buyback payments. You can use CatchCoins to purchase any items on our marketplace, or convert them to cash through your dashboard with no fees. CatchCoins maintain 1:1 value with USD and never expire.
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="How is market value determined?">
    We use real-time sales data from over a dozen major marketplaces to determine the current fair market value of each card or product.
  </AccordionItem>
  
  <AccordionItem title="How do I redeem my catchback or royalty?">
    Simply log in to your account, select the eligible item, and request redemption. For royalties, payouts in CatchCoins are processed automatically after each resale.
  </AccordionItem>
</Accordion>
